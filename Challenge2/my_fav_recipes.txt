Spaghetti Carbonara
Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta
- 2 eggs
- 50g Parmesan cheese
- 50g Pecorino cheese
- Salt and pepper
Instructions:
1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
3. Beat the eggs and mix with the cheeses.
4. Combine the spaghetti with pancetta, then toss with the egg mixture until creamy.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
Cuisine Type: Italian
Taste: Savory, Creamy
Prep Time: 20 minutes
Reviews: 100
